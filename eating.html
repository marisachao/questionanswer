EATING

Whether you're on campus or exploring the surrounding area, St. Louis has a wide variety of restaurants to check out. Washington University's food ranks highly among college options, so students should be sure to try the many campus eateries. Additionally, the school is conveniently located within walking distance of some great restaurants, and is also only a short drive away from other areas with thriving restaurant scenes.
