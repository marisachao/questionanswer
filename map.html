MAP

Washington University's main campus is located in the suburbs of St. Louis. The size of the campus is very manageable as it takes only 15 minutes to walk from one end to the other. Students can find their way to particular buildings using the school's map app which is a function of the WUSTL app. Washington University's campus also lies next to the Delmar Loop and Forest Park. The Loop is a great place to go out to eat or do some shopping. Forest Park is the perfect place to enjoy a nice day outside. Numerous museums and the city's zoo can also be found within the park.


Main Campus Forest Park The Loop
Description Students take classes on main campus. Besides classrooms, the campus also features numerous places to eat, a bookstore, a library, and research labs. Forest Park is one of the nation's largest urban parks. Students enjoy running through the park when the weather is nice. During winter, students also enjoy ice skating and sledding down Art Hill. During their free time, students can visit the museums or the zoo-all of which have free admission. The Delmar Loop has a wide range of restaurants. It is also a fun place to head to at night on the weekends, and students can also take a break with shopping on the Loop.
