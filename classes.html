CLASSES

Washington University in St. Louis offers 1,500 courses within over 90 fields of study. Students are given the freedom to explore a wide range of fields during their time at the university. With such a variety of classes, each student is bound to find something they are passionate about.

Advising Students can contact their 4-year academic advisor, visit the Dean of the Day, or see a pre-professional advisor Business students can make an appointment with their advisor or talk to an advisor during walk-in hours in Simon Hall Sam Fox School has its own Career Services office where students can receive one-on-one advising Engineering students have access to their four-year advisor as well as faculty advisors who can help select courses and health professions advising.

Additional Academic Support

  • Peer Led Team Learning (PLTL)
    • small group peer learning
    • offered for General Chemistry and Calculus
  • Cornerstone
    • academic peer mentoring
    • help with studying and test-taking skills
  • Calculus Help Room
    • graduate student teaching assistants for calculus courses
    • located in Lopata
  • The Writing Center and The Speaking Studio
    • attend workshops
    • work one-on-one for assitance with papers and presentations
